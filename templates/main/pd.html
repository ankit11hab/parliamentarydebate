About

The winds of change are blowing in IIT Guwahati campus on the banks of the majestic River Brahmaputra and IIT Guwahati is thrilled to invite you to join us for the upcoming Parliamentary debate event which is going to happen from 2nd -5th February, 2023. This is your chance to be part of the action and make your voice heard during the debate. Whether you are a student, an alumnus, or an enthusiastic debater, everyone is welcome to this unique opportunity to witness the heated discourse between intelligent minds. The excitement is building up, so be sure to mark your calendar and join us for this thrilling event!
